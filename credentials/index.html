Transparency

What We Do Not Claim

Viscera Energy does not hold ISO 9001, ISO 14001, or ISO 45001 certifications. We do not hold API manufacturing licenses. We do not claim these — and any vendor that asks will find our documentation consistent with this statement. Our credibility rests on the OEM agreements we hold, the credentials above, and the projects we have executed.

This is not a limitation — it is an accurate statement of standing. Any supplier that claims certifications they do not hold creates liability for the client that relies on them.
