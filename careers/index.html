Who We Are

A Technical Organisation
That Stays Small by Choice

Viscera Energy is building a team of engineers and commercial professionals with deep energy sector experience. We are a small, technically-led organisation — every hire matters.

The company was founded by Modupe Famurewa, FNSE, on the principle that OEM authority and engineering operations competence belong in the same organisation. That principle extends to how we hire: we look for people who have done the work, know the equipment, and understand the operating environment — not generalists who can learn on the job at a client's expense.

We operate across Nigeria and West and Central Africa, with a Texas affiliate supporting international business. Field assignments are a reality of this work — the projects are where the value is, and the team travels to deliver them.

If you are an experienced energy engineer, procurement specialist, or business development professional who wants to work in a technically honest environment with real client responsibility, we are interested in hearing from you.

Applications

We Welcome Unsolicited Applications

If you have the experience and want to work with a technically serious energy company, we want to hear from you — vacancy or not.

Energy Engineers
Process, pipeline, mechanical, instrumentation, and electrical engineers with upstream or midstream experience. Minimum ten years' field experience in the energy sector.
Procurement Specialists
Equipment procurement professionals with direct OEM or vendor management experience. Understanding of trade finance, LCs, and international supply chain is a strong advantage.
Business Development
Commercial professionals with existing relationships in West or Central African energy — operators, IOCs, NOCs, or EPCs. Technical credibility alongside commercial capability preferred.
Technical Operations
Operations and maintenance engineers with rotating or static equipment backgrounds. Experience in multi-country deployments across Sub-Saharan Africa is directly relevant.

How to Apply

Send your CV and a brief note on what you could bring to:

Email: sales@visceraenergy.com
Subject line: Application — [Your Discipline]

Applications are reviewed personally. If your background is relevant to what we are building, we will respond.

Where We Work

Ikoyi, Lagos — and the Field

HQ
Lagos, Nigeria

Viscera Energy is headquartered at 186 Awolowo Road, Ikoyi, Lagos — one of the city's established business addresses. Day-to-day commercial, procurement, and technical coordination operates from this office.

US
Texas, USA (Affiliate)

The Texas affiliate supports international procurement and business development. Some roles with an international commercial focus may require periodic US engagement.

WA
West Africa

Active project markets include Nigeria, Ghana, Equatorial Guinea, Senegal, Cameroon, and Ivory Coast. Project engineers and procurement leads will deploy to client sites and supplier facilities across the region.

CA
Central Africa and Gulf

Angola, Cameroon, and Kuwait represent active business development markets. Applicants should be comfortable with multi-country assignments on a project basis.

Viscera Energy is based in Ikoyi, Lagos, Nigeria, with a Texas affiliate. We work across West and Central Africa. Applicants should be comfortable with field assignments across multiple countries. We do not support relocation to Lagos for junior roles. For senior hires, relocation support is discussed case by case.
