OEM-authorised supply with factory documentation, SONCAP compliance, and full certification trace. The chain of custody from factory to field — unbroken.

Why OEM-Led Procurement Matters

The Document Is the Asset

When a valve or compressor fails in the field, the question is never just "what failed?" It is "who supplied it, under what authority, and where is the paperwork?" A project that cannot answer those questions in under an hour has a liability it may not have priced.

Viscera Energy sources directly from OEM agreements. This means every item we supply is traceable to the factory that made it — with the material test certificates, conformance certificates, Factory Acceptance Test reports, and calibration records to prove it. Not copies. Not secondary documentation. The originals.

For operators and EPCs working in regulated environments — NUPRC-regulated Nigerian upstream, IFC-financed infrastructure, IOC-led FEED projects — this matters at every gate review. The Viscera advantage is simple: we are the authority on the equipment we supply, not a step removed from it.

Our OEM network spans thirty manufacturers across twelve countries — including Sweden, Austria, Switzerland, Spain, Denmark, Poland, Turkey, Italy, USA, Germany, India and China. Each relationship is a direct agreement: distribution, agency, or sole authorisation. When you buy through Viscera, you buy at source.

Documentation Standards

The Paper That Protects the Project

Every supply item is accompanied by documentation that is complete, traceable, and legally defensible. Not a summary — the originals.

📋
Material Test Certificates (MTCs)

EN 10204 3.1 and 3.2 certificates from the mill or foundry for all pressure-retaining materials. Provided for every valve body, pipe, fitting and structural element.

✅
Certificate of Conformance (CoC)

Manufacturer's declaration that goods conform to the applicable specification, standard and purchase order requirements. Signed by the manufacturer's quality representative.

🏭
Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) Reports

Factory test records for valves, compressors, generators and instrumentation — including witnessed and unwitnessed test protocols. Third-party inspection coordination available on request.

🛃
SONCAP and NCS-PAAR

Standards Organisation of Nigeria Conformity Assessment Programme (SONCAP) certificates and Nigeria Customs Service Pre-Arrival Assessment Reports (NCS-PAAR) — required for Nigerian importation — coordinated as part of the supply process.

Procurement Process

From Specification to Site

01
Specify

Technical requirement review — equipment type, standard, pressure class, material, and documentation requirements agreed upfront.

02
Quote

OEM-sourced quotation with full commercial and technical data sheet package. Lead times and factory test schedules confirmed.

03
FAT

Factory Acceptance Testing — witnessed or unwitnessed. Third-party inspection coordinated where specified.

04
Ship

Export packing, shipping coordination, and export documentation including packing lists and commercial invoices.

05
Clear

SONCAP, NCS-PAAR and import clearance support. Delivery to port, warehouse or project site.

06
Deliver

Full documentation handover — MTCs, CoCs, FAT reports, calibration records and warranty documentation.
