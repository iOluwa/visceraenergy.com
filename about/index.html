About Viscera Energy

Founded 2012. Direct OEM authority across twelve countries. Engineering depth built over twenty years in Sub-Saharan Africa's energy sector.

Company Story

Built on a Clear Proposition

2012 Founded

Viscera Energy Operations and Maintenance Limited was founded in 2012 on a straightforward proposition — the most credible partner to operate and maintain energy equipment is the partner authorised to supply it. The business was established with an OEM-led approach from the outset: building direct factory relationships rather than secondary distribution channels, and combining supply authority with the engineering depth to commission, operate, and maintain what was supplied.

The company is founded and led by Modupe Famurewa, a Fellow of the Nigerian Society of Engineers with over 20 years of energy infrastructure experience across Sub-Saharan Africa. That technical foundation underpins every commercial relationship and every scope of work the company undertakes.

Operations span upstream oil and gas, midstream, power generation, water infrastructure and industrial process facilities. Active markets include Nigeria, Ghana, Angola, Cameroon, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Equatorial Guinea and Kuwait. The company holds direct OEM agreements with thirty manufacturers across twelve countries — enabling certified equipment supply, engineering integration, and operations support across the full infrastructure lifecycle.

Viscera Energy is an approved NUPRC service provider, registered on SAP Ariba, and holds a D&B DUNS number — enabling participation in international procurement frameworks and global vendor qualification processes.

Group Structure

Two Registered Entities. One Operating Standard.

This domain covers the energy operations and maintenance entities only. Both entities share a unified technical team, OEM network, and operating framework.

NG
Viscera Energy Operations and Maintenance Limited

Incorporated in Nigeria. CAC RC 1393891. Nigerian-registered operating entity with Standard Chartered and Stanbic IBTC banking relationships. NUPRC OGISP permit holder. SAP Ariba registered supplier. D&B DUNS issued. Primary contracting entity for Nigeria and West African operations.

US
Viscera Energy O&M LLC

Registered in Texas, USA (Delaware-origin). EIN issued. USD-denominated contracting capability for international procurement frameworks and global OEM counterparties. Enables participation in tenders requiring US legal presence. Affiliate entity supporting international business development across the Americas and global energy majors.
