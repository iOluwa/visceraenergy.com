About Viscera Energy

OEM Authority.
Engineering Depth.
Field Integrity.

Viscera Energy Operations and Maintenance Limited was founded in 2012 on a straightforward proposition — the most credible partner to operate and maintain energy equipment is the partner authorised to supply it. We hold direct OEM agreements with thirty manufacturers across twelve countries, enabling us to deliver certified equipment, engineering integration, and operations support across the full infrastructure lifecycle.

Operations span upstream oil and gas, midstream, power generation, water infrastructure and industrial process facilities across West and Central Africa. We are an approved NUPRC service provider, registered on SAP Ariba, and hold a D&B DUNS number — enabling participation in international procurement frameworks and global vendor qualification processes.

Our Services

Three Lifecycle Pillars

Source. Deploy. Operate. End-to-end infrastructure delivery under direct OEM authority.

01
📦

Source
Procurement & Supply

OEM-authorised supply with factory documentation, SONCAP compliance, and full certification trace. The chain of custody from factory to field — unbroken.

  • Valve and actuator packages (gate, globe, ball, butterfly, control)
  • Compressors, pumps and rotating equipment
  • Line pipe, steel sections and consumables
  • HVAC systems and balancing valves
  • Power generation equipment (diesel, solar)
  • Instrumentation and metering (CTMS, PRMS)
  • SONCAP and NCS-PAAR clearance support
  • FAT coordination and inspection
02
⚙️

Deploy
Engineering & Integration

Process design basis, P&IDs, modular system specification and CTMS packages delivered with global engineering partners holding the original design basis.

  • Oil and gas facility engineering (fixed, floating, onshore, offshore)
  • Pipeline and piping design (ASME B31.4, B31.8)
  • CTMS and PRMS system design and procurement
  • Modular and pre-fabricated unit specifications
  • EPC support and package management
  • Technical and manpower supply services
  • Procurement and supply chain management
  • OEM material supply and interface support
03
🔧

Operate
Operations, Maintenance & Integrity

Instrumentation and control, rotating and static equipment, pipeline and process integrity — delivered under OEM technical authority. Capability actively expanding.

  • Valve inspection, maintenance and repair (OEM-certified)
  • Rotating equipment operations and maintenance
  • Pipeline integrity management (API 570, ASME B31.4)
  • Static equipment inspection (API 510, 653)
  • HVAC commissioning and maintenance
  • Field services and aftermarket support
  • Digital pipeline integrity solutions
  • Asset management and operations support

02
Regulatory Credentials That Pass Due Diligence
